Apple Inc.

About Apple

Apple Inc. is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Cupertino, California, that designs, develops, and sells consumer electronics, computer software, and online services. It is considered one of the Big Tech technology companies, alongside Amazon, Google, Microsoft, and Facebook.

History

  1. Founded by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne on April 1, 1976.
  2. Released the first Apple computer, the Apple I, in 1976.
  3. Launched the Macintosh in 1984, introducing the graphical user interface to a wider audience.
  4. Introduced the iPod in 2001, revolutionizing the music industry.
  5. Launched the iPhone in 2007, transforming the smartphone market.
  6. Released the iPad in 2010, creating a new category of mobile devices.
